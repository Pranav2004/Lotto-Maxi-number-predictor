Lotto Max

Lotto Max draws take place every Tuesday and Friday evening at 10:30pm E.T., with jackpots of at least $10 million on offer.

Lotto Max - How to Play

The aim of the game in Lotto Max is to match seven numbers from 1 to 50 to the winning numbers that are drawn on the night. You can choose your own numbers or get a Quick Pick, and you will then receive two more random sets of numbers. This gives you three chances to win the jackpot.

If you get all seven numbers the same, you win the multimillion-dollar jackpot. There are nine different prize categories to play for in Lotto Max – be sure to check the full set of winning numbers for each game, including the Bonus Ball, so you don’t miss out on a prize.

Atlantic Canada

The Atlantic Lottery Corporation operates in Newfoundland & Labrador, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island and Nova Scotia. There are a couple of games you can add on to Lotto Max here.

Twist uses the winning Lotto Max numbers but there’s a slight tweak to how you win prizes which improves your odds. In Tag, you must match numbers from 0 to 9 in the right order. Both games have jackpots of $100,000.

Quebec

Lottery games in Quebec are run by Loto-Quebec. You can take part in the province’s Extra game alongside Lotto Max and win $1 million if you match seven digits in their exact positions.

Quebec Max is a game that can be played on its own and works in a similar way to Lotto Max. There are 14 prizes of $1 million, plus a jackpot of $2 million, in every draw.

British Columbia

The British Columbia Lottery Corporation has a version of Extra that can be played on top of many of the games you will find in the region, including Lotto Max.

The jackpot is worth $500,000 and you only have to match four numbers to win. What’s more, the prize is fixed per winner so you won’t have to share even if there are multiple players who do the same.

Ontario

Ontario is Canada’s most populated province and has been home to many of Lotto Max’s biggest winners. It is also the place where you can add Encore to your ticket for another chance to become a millionaire.

To win Encore’s top prize you need to match seven digits in the correct order, but there are also a host of other ways to get payouts. In fact, there are 23 different prize categories in total.

Western Canada

The Western Canada Lottery Corporation administers games in Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta, Nunavut, Northwest Territories and Yukon.

You’ll find a Western Max game here which puts a $2 million jackpot up for grabs twice a week, plus a version of Extra where you can win up to $250,000 by matching numbers in exactly the right order.
